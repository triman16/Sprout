Meeting 1: How to bootstrap your startup

In this meeting, we learned from the founder of Saahas, Wilma Rodrigues, how she started her non-profit organization with minimal resources and scaled it to a successful social enterprise. She shared her insights on the following topics:

  • How to identify a problem and find a solution that aligns with your passion and values
  • How to leverage your network and community to get support and feedback
  • How to manage your finances and operations efficiently and effectively
  • How to overcome the challenges and risks of bootstrapping your startup
  • How to measure your impact and communicate your story

Meeting 2: How to create a circular economy with your waste

In this meeting, we learned from the founder of Switcheko, Akshay Deshpande, how he created a platform that enables people to trade their waste for eco-friendly products. He shared his insights on the following topics:

  • How to find a market opportunity and validate your idea
  • How to design and develop your product and service
  • How to acquire and retain your customers and partners
  • How to scale and grow your business and impact
  • How to deal with the challenges and opportunities of the circular economy

Meeting 3: How to sell and buy preloved items online

In this meeting, we learned from the team of Free up, how they created a platform that allows people to sell and buy clothes, beauty and home items that are new or preloved. They shared their insights on the following topics:

  • How to conduct market research and customer segmentation
  • How to build and test your minimum viable product (MVP)
  • How to optimize your user interface and user experience (UI/UX)
  • How to market and promote your platform and brand
  • How to handle the logistics and transactions of your platform
