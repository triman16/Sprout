Resources

This page shows links to other helpful sites, books, podcasts, and videos that might be useful for students who are curious about learning the green sector. These resources are carefully handpicked for them only and are mostly from Indian sources.

Sites

  • India Climate Dialogue: A site that provides articles, reports, and resources on climate change policy, negotiations, and action in India.
  • Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change (MoEFCC): The official website of the Indian government ministry responsible for environmental protection, forests, and climate change.
  • International Solar Alliance (ISA): A global alliance led by India that aims to promote solar energy deployment and ensure energy access for all.

Books

  • Green Growth: India's strategy for Green Economy by ClearIAS: A book that explains the concept of green growth and the various initiatives taken by the Indian government to achieve it.
  • Green Computing: Tools and Techniques for Saving Energy, Money, and Resources by Bud E. Smith and Michael S. Erbschloe: A book that covers various aspects of green computing, such as hardware, software, data centers, cloud computing, and mobile devices.
  • Green Software Engineering: The Future of Software Development by Andreas Betz, Stefan Naumann, and Veronika Rehn-Sonigo: A book that introduces the concept of green software engineering and provides practical guidance on how to apply it.

Podcasts

  • Down To Earth - The Green Podcast: A podcast that discusses environmental news, challenges, and solutions through engaging interviews and discussions.
  • India Spend - Environment Podcast: A podcast that features investigative reports and discussions on environmental issues in India.
  • The Sustainability Podcast by TERI: A podcast that features experts talking about sustainability challenges and solutions across various sectors in India.

Videos

  • The Energy and Resources Institute (TERI) YouTube Channel: A channel that provides educational videos on sustainability, renewable energy, and environmental issues.
  • World Wildlife Fund (WWF) India YouTube Channel: A channel that features inspiring stories and documentaries about wildlife conservation and environmental protection in India.
  • Greenpeace India YouTube Channel: A channel that showcases campaign videos and documentaries highlighting environmental challenges and advocating for solutions.
