Connect

Connect is a platform for users to connect with community based on their interests and career goals. This is a forum for ongoing discussion on different green job topics, like sustainable fashion group or responsible consumption group etc, just like discord, so that people can come discuss, ask questions, leave them for others to answer etc.

Groups

  • Sustainable Fashion: A group for people who are interested in the environmental and social impacts of the fashion industry.
  • Responsible Consumption: A group for people who want to learn how to reduce their ecological footprint and live more sustainably.
  • Green Energy: A group for people who are passionate about renewable energy sources and technologies.
